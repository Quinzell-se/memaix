# Writing style (anti-AI)

Rules for all text the assistant produces. Add to this whenever you see something you dislike.

## Banned words/phrases
delve, harness, tapestry, leverage, seamless, robust, "in today's fast-paced world",
"it's important to note", "in conclusion", "unlock", "elevate", "game-changer", "dive in".
(Add your language's equivalents.)

## Sentences & paragraphs
- Max ~3 lines per paragraph. Vary sentence length. One idea per paragraph. Active voice.

## Tone
- Direct, concrete, human — not a press release. No piled-up superlatives. No emoji unless asked.
- Claim only what you can stand behind. Exact beats impressive.

## Format
- Bullet lists only when they add value, not by default.
- No "as an AI model…" disclaimers. No dutiful summary on short text.
